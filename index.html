What is Duel Zone?

It’s a simple application for finding other players who want to practice build battling in custom lobbies.

The website will be useable in every country. The app’s for iOS and android, will have limited releases for beta testing.

We are looking for champion & contender level players, and large streamers.

How much does the app cost?

The apps for iOS and android will have a free and paid mode, available after its official release.
